Cointime Price 概念

时间成交量加权后的实现价格，公式: subset(f2/cumsum(f1), "2010-09-01")

f2: 累计已实现的销毁币块总价值

= cumsum(BTC price × Coin Blocks Destroyed)

f1: 累计的存储币块总量

= Coin Blocks Created - Coin Blocks Destroyed
